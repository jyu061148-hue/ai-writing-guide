# Word Swaps — AI Tells and Human Alternatives

## Tier 1: Biggest Red Flags

These words are heavily overused by ChatGPT/Claude and are the first things detectors and professors look for.

| AI Word/Phrase | Why It's Flagged | Human Alternatives |
|---|---|---|
| delve (into) | #1 most overused LLM word | look at, examine, explore, dig into |
| moreover / furthermore / additionally | Robotic transitions | also, and, plus, on top of that — or just cut it |
| it's important to note | Filler hedge | just state the thing directly |
| in conclusion / in summary | Template closer | drop it — readers know it's the end |
| groundbreaking / transformative / revolutionary | Vague hype | say *what* specifically changed |
| leverage / harness / foster | Corporate AI-speak | use, build, encourage, grow |
| tapestry / realm / landscape | Abstract fluff | say what you actually mean (field, area, space) |
| at its core | Empty filler | cut it, or "fundamentally" if you must |
| comprehensive | Overused qualifier | thorough, detailed, full — or cut it |
| pivotal | AI favorite | key, important, central |
| underscore | AI favorite | highlight, show, emphasize |
| noteworthy | Stiff | worth noting, interesting, stands out |
| commendable | Sounds like a performance review | impressive, solid, strong |
| embark (on) | AI loves this word | start, begin, take on |
| showcasing | AI-speak | showing, demonstrating |
| aligns (with) | Corporate tone | fits, matches, goes with |

## Tier 2: Overused Phrases

| AI Phrase | Human Alternative |
|---|---|
| in today's fast-paced world | cut entirely — it's a cliché |
| in the ever-evolving landscape of... | cut entirely |
| in the realm of | in [specific field] |
| sheds light on | shows, reveals, clarifies |
| gain valuable insights | learn, find out, understand |
| plays a crucial/significant role | matters for, affects, shapes |
| a testament to | shows, proves |
| offers valuable perspectives | — just present the perspectives |
| it's not X, it's Y | rewrite — this is a known AI pattern |
| by [doing X], you can [achieve Y] | vary the structure |
| treasure trove of information | cut — pure fluff |
| deep dive | detailed look, closer look |
| chaos into clarity | cut — AI cliché |

## Tier 3: Hedging & Softening (overuse is the problem)

These are fine occasionally but AI uses them *constantly*:

- "certainly"
- "arguably"
- "notably"
- "essentially"
- "generally speaking"
- "to some extent"
- "from a broader perspective"
- "it is worth mentioning"
- "based on the information provided"
- "remember that"

**Rule of thumb:** If you can delete the hedge and the sentence still works, delete it.

## Model-Specific Tendencies

### ChatGPT
- Heavy on "delve," "tapestry," "landscape," "embark"
- Loves numbered lists with bold headers
- Often overly enthusiastic ("Great question!")
- Defaults to "It's not just about X; it's about Y" framing

### Claude
- More likely to use "notably," "arguably," "nuanced"
- Tends toward careful hedging and qualifications
- Can be overly thorough — says the same thing three ways
- Sometimes overly self-aware ("As an AI...")
